Bunny Cafe SF

Figma UI/UX Web Design

Designed and maintaining the online site and services for Bunny Cafe San Francisco.

About

I had the opportunity to design this website for a family friend launching San Francisco's first bunny cafe. At the time, I had no professional web design experience and little knowledge of e-commerce. With her trust and a little bit of elbow grease, I delivered a sleek, functional site equipped with the features her business needed—and it was met with enthusiastic approval.

Project Details

  • Created a clean, sleek design with a custom color palette
  • Rendered a product-mockup for a customizable membership card
  • Managing all online services for the business, including ecommerce and reservation systems.
