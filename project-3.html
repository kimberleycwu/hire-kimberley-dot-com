Wicked 'That's My Line' Re-Creation

3D Modeling Blender Unreal Engine

Building and rendering a stylized version of the infamous 'That's My Line' advert.

About

This is the culmination of work done for 3D Modeling/Animation with Michael Walsh at Texas A&M University. The goal is to recreate a commercial of your choice, so I chose the infamous Target advert that was released as part of the promotional materials for the first 'Wicked' movie.

Project Details

  • Planned and executed a full animation pipeline, managing asset creation, scene layout, and real-time rendering workflows.
  • Organized and integrated character models, textures, and rigs across Blender and Unreal Engine to ensure a consistent production pipeline.
  • Coordinated project planning, shot lists, and asset tracking to simulate feature-animation production practices.
