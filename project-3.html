Wicked 'That's My Line' Re-Creation

3D Modeling Blender Unreal Engine

Building and rendering a stylized version of the infamous 'That's My Line' advert.

About

This is the culmination of work done for 3D Modeling/Animation with Michael Walsh at Texas A&M University. The goal is to recreate a commercial of your choice, so I chose the infamous Target advert that was released as part of the promotional materials for the first 'Wicked' movie. Ah Aaa-Aaa Ahhh!!! (Fluorishes broomstick.)

Project Details

  • Recreating a commercial from scratch, handling project planning (shot lists, outlines), asset creation, and animation to produce a fully realized real-time sequence.
  • Modeling, rigging, texturing, and animating characters and assets in Blender.
  • Integrating assets and character rigs into Unreal Engine for layout, blocking, and real-time rendering.
